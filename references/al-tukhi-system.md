# Al-Tukhi (الطوخي) — System & Teachings Reference
## Compiled from available sources + cross-references in Shadrach & other texts

### Identity
- **Full Name:** Abdul Fattah al-Sayed al-Tawukhi (عبدالفتاح السيد الطوخي)
- **Also known as:** Al-Toukhi, Al-Tawukhi
- **Known Works:**
  - *Ahkam al-Hakim fi Ilm al-Tanjim* (أحكام الحكيم في علم التنجيم) — Rulings of the Wise in the Science of Astrology (4+ volumes)
  - *al-Bediah wa al-Nehayah* (البدية والنهاية) — The Beginning and the End (referenced in Shadrach bibliography, Beirut: al-Maktabah al-Tahqafiah, 1980)
  - *Kitab al-Kanz al-Maknun* (كتاب الكنز المكنون) — The Hidden Treasure
- **Tradition:** Islamic occult sciences (*al-ulum al-gharibah*), combining:
  - Traditional Arabic/Persian astrology (*ahkam*)
  - *'Ilm al-huruf* (letter science) after al-Buni
  - Talismanic magic (*awfaq* / magic squares)
  - Lunar mansion astrology (*manazil al-qamar*)
  - Astrological elections (*ikhtiyarat*)

### Core Teachings

#### 1. Astrological Judgment (Ahkam)
Al-Tukhi's primary focus is on *astrological judgment* — using planetary positions, aspects, and house placements to determine the nature and outcome of events. His system is:
- **Tropical-based** but incorporates *fixed-star* considerations
- **House-dominant**: The condition of the house ruler determines the outcome more than sign placement
- **Aspect-based**: Primary aspects (conjunction, sextile, square, trine, opposition) with orbs varying by planet

#### 2. The Operative Temperament
Central to Al-Tukhi's system is the concept of *mizaj* (temperament) of each celestial moment:
- Each planet has a temperament: Hot/Cold × Dry/Moist
- Each lunar mansion has a temperament
- The *operative nature* of any moment = combination of planetary hour ruler + mansion nature + day ruler + current aspects
- Actions should align with the temperament: e.g., Martian hours for cutting/separating, Venusian hours for love/union

#### 3. Lunar Mansion System (Manazil al-Qamar)
Al-Tukhi follows the standard 28-mansion system but with specific emphasis on:
- **Spirit regents** (*ruhaniyyat*) governing each mansion — both angelic and jinn
- **Suited operations** per mansion (what to do, what to avoid)
- **Qur'anic ayat** prescribed for each mansion's operations
- **Abjad letters** associated with each mansion
- The specific *nature* (hot/cold/dry/moist) of each mansion and its effect on the operation

#### 4. Talismanic Elections (Ikhtiyarat)
Al-Tukhi provides detailed rules for electing times to make talismans:
- Planetary hour must be sympathetic to the talisman's purpose
- Moon must be in a mansion compatible with the work
- Moon must not be void-of-course
- The ascending sign must be appropriate
- Day of week must match the planetary ruler

#### 5. The Science of Letters
Following al-Buni's tradition:
- Each Arabic letter has a numerical value (abjad)
- Each letter corresponds to a planet, element, and spiritual hierarchy
- Letters are combined to form divine names and *awfaq* (magic squares)
- The *taksir* (breakdown) and *basṭ* (expansion) of divine names reveals hidden powers

### Key Practical Rules

1. **Mars in the 7th** → disputes with partners, avoid contracts
2. **Saturn in the 4th** → delays in home/family matters, ancestral karma active
3. **Venus in the 10th** → public favor, career advancement through charm
4. **Moon in Mansion 19 (Al-Shaulah)** → severance, root-cutting, breaking bonds
5. **Mercury combust** → confusion in communication, don't sign documents
6. **Jupiter in 1st** → expansion of self, good for beginnings (especially in Cancer, his exaltation)
7. **Sun □ Neptune** → fog of illusion, things not as they appear, trust intuition

### Cross-Reference: Shadrach on Al-Tukhi
In *Magic Squares and the Tree of Life*, Nineveh Shadrach lists Al-Toukhi's *al-Bediah wa al-Nehayah* in his bibliography — suggesting Al-Tukhi's work on magic squares (*awfaq*) was influential enough to be cited by a modern practitioner.

### Diagnostic Framework for Daily Readings

When interpreting a day through Al-Tukhi's system:
1. **Day Ruler** — What is the base temperament of the day?
2. **Current Planetary Hour** — What spirit governs this moment?
3. **Lunar Mansion** — What is the Moon's nature and what does it prescribe?
4. **Dominant Aspect** — What single celestial relationship defines the moment?
5. **Planetary Condition** — Which planets are in dignity/fall/detriment?
6. **Operative Advice** — Based on temperament, what actions are suited/avoided?

### Ayat Prescribed by Mansion Type
- **Protective operations**: Ayat al-Kursi (2:255), Al-Falaq (113), Al-Nas (114)
- **Severance operations**: Al-Ikhlas (112), Al-Falaq (113)
- **Love/Union operations**: Surah Yusuf (12), Surah Al-Rum (30:21)
- **Prosperity operations**: Ayat al-Dayn (2:282), Surah Al-Waqi'ah (56)
- **Healing operations**: Surah Al-Fatihah (1), Ayat al-Shifa (9:14, 16:69, 17:82, 26:80)

### Mansion Temperament Quick Reference
- **Hot & Dry** (Martian) → Cutting, protection, destruction of enemies
- **Cold & Dry** (Saturnine) → Binding, restriction, ancestral work
- **Hot & Moist** (Jovial/Venereal) → Growth, love, prosperity, expansion
- **Cold & Moist** (Lunar) → Dreams, emotions, divination, spirits
- **Air/Mercurial** → Intellect, communication, travel, commerce
