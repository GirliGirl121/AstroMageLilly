## Book 2: Conjunctions @ The Speed of Light

**Analysis of all planetary conjunctions (2+ planets) and how their energies blend.**

- Sun + Moon = New Moon conjunction, emotional tension
- Sun + Mars = Combust Mars, anger issues
- Moon + Venus = Artistic, romantic sensitivity
- Mercury + Jupiter = Wise speech, astrological ability
- Saturn + Rahu = Deep obsession, karmic burden
- Venus + Mars = Passion combined with drive

Key principle: When planets conjoin, they exchange energies — the stronger planet dominates but both are colored by the other's nature.

## Book 3: Aspects @ The Speed of Light

**Planetary aspects in the Vedic system.**

- All planets aspect the 7th house from themselves (opposition in Western terms)
- Mars aspects 4th and 8th houses additionally
- Jupiter aspects 5th and 9th houses additionally
- Saturn aspects 3rd and 10th houses additionally
- Rahu/Ketu aspect 5th and 9th houses additionally

Aspect strength depends on:
- Applying vs separating
- Sign-based dignity of the aspecting planet
- Whether the aspect crosses kendra/trikona/dusthana houses

### How to Determine Starting Dasha
1. Find the tropical Moon's nakshatra (divide longitude by 13°20')
2. Find which planetary lord rules that nakshatra
3. Balance remaining years based on how far the Moon has progressed through the nakshatra
4. Proceed through the dasha sequence

### Sub-Periods (Bhuktis)
Each mahadasha has sub-periods of all 9 planets in the same sequence, proportional to their year count.

### Dasha Interpretation
- During a planet's dasha, that planet's house placement, sign, aspects, and conjunctions become activated
- The dasha lord acts through its house position and the house of the planet it aspects
- Malefic dashas bring challenges; benefic dashas bring opportunities
- Transits through natal positions during relevant dashas trigger events

## Additional KRS Teachings

### Nakshatra-Based Prediction
Each nakshatra has specific qualities, deities, and ganas (Deva/Manushya/Rakshasa) that color the planets placed within them.

### Divisional Charts
- D-1 (Rasi): Main birth chart
- D-9 (Navamsha): Marriage and spiritual path
- D-10 (Dashamsha): Career and public life
- D-3 (Drekkana): Siblings and courage
- D-7 (Saptamsha): Children
- D-12 (Dwadashamsha): Parents and ancestry

### Rahu & Ketu in Practice
- Rahu amplifies whatever it touches — but creates illusion
- Ketu dissolves whatever it touches — but grants liberation
- Both are always retrograde, always 7 houses apart
- Rahu is most powerful in Taurus, Gemini, Virgo, Libra, Sagittarius, Pisces
- Ketu is most powerful in Scorpio (its own sign)
