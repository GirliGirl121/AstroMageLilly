✨ Aspects
SymbolAspectMeaning
☌Conjunction (0°)Energies merge — intense, focused, blended
⚹Sextile (60°)Supportive opportunity — gentle, requires effort
□Square (90°)Tension & friction — challenges that drive growth
△Trine (120°)Natural harmony — talents that flow effortlessly
☍Opposition (180°)Polarization — balance between two forces
⚺Quincunx (150°)Adjustment needed — awkward, requires adaptation
⚻Quintile (72°)Creative gift — subtle talent, inspiration
⚼Semi-sextile (30°)Mild connection — small opportunities, awareness
☌ Conjunction: Can be harmonious or challenging depending on the planets involved.
□ Square vs △ Trine: Squares push you to grow; Trines give natural ease. Both are needed.
Orbs: The tighter the aspect (smaller orb), the stronger its influence. Major orbs are 6–10°.
